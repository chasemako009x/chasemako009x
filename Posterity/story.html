PART 1 OF THE STORY

If you prefer to watch the full walkthrough rather than reading the story play the video


The Last of Us is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The game, originally released for the PlayStation 3 in 2013, has been praised for its deep narrative, character development, and emotional impact. Below, I'll outline the full storyline, divided according to the game's chapters, reflecting the perspectives of the main characters, Joel and Ellie. Please note, this will include major spoilers for the entire game.



Prologue

  • Location:Austin, Texas
  • Perspective:Joel
  • Summary: The game opens with Joel returning home from work, interacting with his daughter Sarah. The outbreak of a mutant Cordyceps fungus rapidly escalates into a pandemic. Joel, his brother Tommy, and Sarah attempt to flee the chaos, but Sarah is tragically killed by a soldier trying to contain the outbreak.


The Quarantine Zone (20 Years Later)

  • Location: Boston, Massachusetts
  • Perspective:Joel
  • Summary: Twenty years after the outbreak, society has collapsed. Joel and his partner Tess operate as smugglers in a militarized quarantine zone. They are tasked with smuggling a girl, Ellie, out of the zone in exchange for a cache of weapons.


The Outskirts

  • Location: Outside the Boston QZ
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary:Joel, Tess, and Ellie journey through the ruins of Boston to reach the drop-off point. They encounter infected and learn that Ellie is immune to the Cordyceps infection, making her potentially key to developing a vaccine.


Bill's Town

  • Location: Lincoln, Massachusetts
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: Joel and Ellie seek out Joel's old acquaintance, Bill, to procure a vehicle. They navigate through traps set for the infected and fend off attackers, eventually leaving with a working car.


Pittsburgh

  • Location: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: Their car is ambushed, leading to a series of conflicts with hostile survivors. They meet Henry and Sam, brothers also trying to find the Fireflies, a militia group believed to be working on a cure.


The Suburbs

  • Location: Pittsburgh outskirts
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: After escaping Pittsburgh, they face more infected and tragic moments with their new allies, leading to heartbreaking losses. The group's dynamics and trust are tested.


Tommy's Dam

  • Location: Jackson County, Wyoming
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: Joel and Ellie find Tommy, Joel's brother, who has established a thriving community near a hydroelectric dam. Joel initially decides to leave Ellie with Tommy but changes his mind after a series of events, choosing to continue the journey with her.


The University

  • Location: Eastern Colorado University
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: Searching for the Fireflies, Joel and Ellie arrive at an abandoned university. They encounter resistance and in the process, Joel is seriously injured.


Lakeside Resort

  • Location: Silver Lake, Colorado
  • Perspective: Ellie
  • Summary: With Joel incapacitated, Ellie becomes the caregiver and protector. She hunts for food and defends against both infected and hostile survivors. Ellie's resourcefulness and determination are highlighted.


Bus Depot

  • Location: Salt Lake City, Utah
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: Joel recovers, and they continue to Salt Lake City. They encounter a herd of giraffes in a serene moment, offering a brief respite from their struggles.


The Firefly Lab

  • Location: Salt Lake City
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: They finally reach the Fireflies, only to discover that creating a vaccine would result in Ellie's death. Unwilling to lose another "daughter," Joel fights his way through the hospital to rescue Ellie, killing many Fireflies, including the lead surgeon.


Epilogue

  • Location: Outskirts of Tommy's settlement
  • Perspective: Joel, with Ellie
  • Summary: As they return to Tommy's community, Ellie expresses doubt about her survival being worth it. Joel lies to her, claiming the Fireflies had stopped looking for a cure, to protect her from feeling her immunity was for nothing.
"The Last of Us" presents a gripping narrative, exploring themes of loss, survival, and the lengths to which individuals will go to protect those they care about. This division of the story by chapter highlights the progression of Joel and Ellie's relationship from strangers to a bond akin to that of a father and daughter, set against the backdrop of a post-apocalyptic world.
